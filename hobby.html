My Hobby

When it comes to my hobby, it is reading. At first im not really into reading, but there is this one novel that suddenly makes me interested and thus making reading as my hobby. That novel is called:



Nirnama is a popular Malaysian epic action-fantasy novel series written by Hilal Asyraf. Set in a fictionalized version of the Malay Archipelago, the story follows a mysterious, nameless warrior who wanders the land defending oppressed commoners and fighting corrupt, tyrannical regimes.


Because he keeps his true identity hidden, the local people name him "Nirnama" (meaning "nameless" in Sanskrit/Malay). The saga combines thrilling martial arts (Silat) action with deep spiritual teachings and Islamic moral values.

Nirnama series currently consist of 5 novels:


1. Nirnama

2. Nirnama: Himpunan Hikayat Tanah Semenanjung (Prequel)

3. Nirnama 2

4. Nirnama Hikayat 5 Laksamana (Prequel to Nirnama 2)

5. Nirnama Hikayat Pengembaraan Ke Timur

There is also a comic version that expands on the lore of the main novel through prequels and side stories:


1. Komik Nirnama: Kelana Pendekar Tanpa Nama — A non-serialized comic that explores the 10 stories of Nirnama's 5-year journey across the peninsula before the events of the original novel.


2. Nirnama: Hikayat Pendekar Tanpa Nama 1&2 — A comic series that visually adapts the core adventures of the nameless warrior in the first novel.

Hilal Asyraf also released "Nirnama Cilik" series that are heavily focused on the audience at the age of 7-12. Each of the book has 10 chapters that is focused on a certain topic and has its own theme that are filled with valuable lesson as well as colorful illustrations. This series takes place after the end of the of Nirnama novel.

The Next Huge Step for Hilal Asyraf

On April 23rd 2026, Wau Animation (The studio behind Ejen Ali) officially enters into partnership with Hilal Asyraf to produce the animation adaptation of Nirnama.

Usamah Zaid, the founder of WAU Animation (the studio behind Ejen Ali), thinks very highly of Hilal Asyraf's Nirnama series. His first main impressions includes, despite the thickness of the novel, he found the novel to be a fast, gripping read that doesn't get boring. He also says that he loves the story's Islamic approach and messaging (dakwah), noting it inspired him to want to become a better Muslim.


While he feels the main Nirnama novel might be slightly heavy for younger kids, he praises spin-offs like Nirnama Cilik (Hikayat Untuk Si Cilik) as a perfectly simplified, visually appealing entry point that builds children's vocabulary with its language glossary. His deep appreciation for the series has even led WAU Animation to partner with the author to adapt the Nirnama novel into an animated format.

On 7th June 2026, Wau Animation released a video on their youtube channel about the "Early Revealing of Nirnama Animation" at PBAKL2026.
