Games

Here are one of the games that i truly enjoy so much!

Developer: ATLUS

This is a story of how people must find unity to elect a new king. Our story takes place in The United Kingdom of Euchronia, where the assassination of the king brings chaos and unrest to the land.


In the midst of this, the protagonist, together with his partner, the fairy Gallica, must find a way to break the curse that has been placed on the prince that the kingdom believes to be dead.


They will discover that in order to achieve their goal, they must participate in the tournament for the throne, and this great task shall require them to ally with many friends and followers of the various tribes inhabiting the world.

Developer: Toby Fox

Deltarune is an episodic RPG set in a parallel universe to Undertale. The story follows a quiet human teenager named Kris and their formerly aggressive classmate Susie. They discover a "Dark World," where they must team up with a dark prince, Ralsei, to seal dark fountains and save the world from an apocalyptic prophecy.

Developer: Adhoc Studios

Dispatch is an episodic superhero workplace comedy video game. You play as Robert Robertson, a disgraced, powerless hero whose high-tech mech-suit is destroyed in battle. Forced to take a desk job as a dispatcher, you manage a team of reformed supervillains while trying to rebuild your suit and reclaim your life.

My favourite anime will forever be:

My Hero Academia follows Izuku Midoriya, a powerless boy living in a society where 80% of the population has superpowers called "Quirks". After catching the attention of the world's greatest hero, he inherits an ultimate ability, gets enrolled in an elite superhero high school, and trains to become the next "Symbol of Peace".
