Who Am I?

My name is Muhammad Nirman Atif bin Mohd Azrool Rizal. I am 20 years old and I live in Gerik, Perak. Believe it or not, i was born in Pahang and grew up in Kudat, Sabah. The reason for me to live in Sabah is because my father got a work offer there so our family had to move out. I am the oldest out of 3 siblings.

To be completely honest, my life isn't that interesting. But at the same time, it isn't that boring either. I met a lot of wonderful people along the way and i will be forever grateful for them.

My Background

I am currently studying in UiTM Kedah branch, pursuing a Diploma in Library Informatics. This course teaches me about cataloging, classification, metadata management, databases, library services, as well as information retrieval.
